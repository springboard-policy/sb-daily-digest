## Northern & Arctic Infrastructure

[Saturday] **[Arviat celebrates being chosen as site of first Inuit-led university in Canada](https://itk.ca/arviat-celebrates-being-chosen-as-site-of-first-inuit-led-university-in-canada/)** — Construction of the main Inuit Nunangat University campus and student residence in Arviat, Nunavut is set to begin this summer with 1,800 truckloads of gravel breaking ground on the site, with the first student cohort targeted for 2030.
