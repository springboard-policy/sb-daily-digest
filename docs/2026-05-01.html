Government of Yukon opens new licensing pathway for U.S.-trained doctors — The Yukon Government has created an expedited licensing pathway specifically for physicians trained in the United States, an explicit response to deteriorating Canada-U.S. relations that is designed to attract American doctors facing political or professional uncertainty under the current U.S. administration.
