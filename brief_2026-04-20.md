   *Flagged in the April 17 brief as a story to watch, this confirms a systemic gap: Nunavut's smallest and most remote hamlets remain highly vulnerable to cascading infrastructure failures, and the absence of mandatory emergency planning frameworks at the hamlet level is a direct policy gap the Government of Nunavut and federal emergency preparedness programs have not yet closed.*
